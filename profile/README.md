# Sirius Quantum

**Building the open quantum ecosystem.**

We are democratising Quantum AI through three pillars:

- **Annotation Network** — 100+ quantum domain experts labelling and validating quantum data at scale
- **Labelling Studio** — purpose-built tooling for quantum circuit and algorithm annotation
- **Simulation Network** — distributed quantum simulation on Apple Silicon, open to all

Our infrastructure is designed to be open, community-owned, and hardware-accessible — so that quantum AI is not locked behind proprietary clouds.
