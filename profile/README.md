# Sirius Quantum

**Building the open quantum ecosystem.**

We are democratising Quantum AI through three pillars:

- **Annotation Network** — 100+ quantum domain experts evaluations and validating quantum data at scale
- ** Re-Lab Studio** — quantum native data re-labeling engine for physical AI models ( visit Hugginface for quantum datasets and kernels) 
- **Simulation Network** — distributed quantum simulation on Apple Silicon, launching soon! 

Our infrastructure is designed to be open, community-owned, and hardware-accessible so that quantum AI is not locked behind proprietary clouds.
